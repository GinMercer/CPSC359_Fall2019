README!!!!!

I do not do the bonus, thus just use 
>make clean         (let the file clean)
>make all           (until there is the file called "kernel8.img")

Put the kernel8.img to SD card, and put the SD card to Pi.

my square will first show at the left top of the screen.
> left			(let my square move left and leave the track)
> right			(let my square move right and leave the track)	
> up			(let my square move up and leave the track)
> down			(let my square move down and leave the track)
> start			(let the track disappear, and restart at the point where we press the start button)
